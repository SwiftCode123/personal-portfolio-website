Project Description


CodePath.org is a non-profit organization focused on improving diversity in tech by providing free, hands-on coding courses, mentorship, and career support for underrepresented students. They offer programs in areas like software engineering, cybersecurity, and mobile app development, aiming to bridge the gap between academic learning and industry demands. CodePath partners with colleges, universities, and leading tech companies to prepare students with practical skills and help them build professional networks, making the tech industry more accessible and equitable.

Project Description


Are you a student struggling to attract attendees for your organization's events through posters and Instagram posts? Do you often miss out on exciting campus events because you only learn about them after they've concluded? ExpertEvents enables organizers to promote events to the entire student population. It allows students to stay informed about campus happenings, effortlessly notifying and inviting friends. Engage in discussions through our in-app community space, and seamlessly integrate events with your calendar app. ExpertEvents keeps you in the loop with real-time notifications.

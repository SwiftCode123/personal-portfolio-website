Girl You Matter

Non-profit website project

Project Description


Girlyoumatter.org is a dynamic web platform designed to empower girls during their transition from middle school to high school. With a user-friendly interface crafted using HTML, CSS, and JS, the site offers a wealth of resources, advice, and support tailored to the unique challenges young girls face during this pivotal time in their lives. From academic guidance to self-esteem boosting tips, Girlyoumatter.org aims to foster confidence, resilience, and a sense of belonging as girls embark on their high school journey
